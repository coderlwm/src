# Privacy Policy — Chord Master

**Last updated:** May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Chord Master** (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use our mobile application on **Android** and **iOS**.

- **App name:** Chord Master  
- **Platforms:** Google Play (Android), Apple App Store (iOS)

By using Chord Master, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

---

## Summary

Chord Master is a **local-first** music creation app. Your chords, scales, tabs, and folders are stored **on your device**. We do **not** operate user accounts, do **not** sell your data, and do **not** use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs in the app.

Limited data may be processed by **Google Play** or the **Apple App Store** when you buy or restore premium features, or when the app checks whether a newer version is available in the store.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Content you create (stored on your device)

The app stores music-related data you enter, including for example:

- Folder names and organization  
- Guitar string chords, piano chords, scales, and chord tabs  
- Chord names, fret positions, and related editor settings  

This data is kept in a **local SQLite database** on your phone or tablet. It is **not** uploaded to our servers because we do not operate backend servers for user content.

### 2. App settings and preferences (stored on your device)

We save preferences locally using on-device storage (for example **SharedPreferences**), such as:

- Light / dark / system theme  
- Theme color  
- Which creation types are enabled in the UI  
- Whether premium features are unlocked on this device  

### 3. Premium purchase status

If you purchase **Premium** (one-time in-app product):

- **Google Play** (Android) or the **Apple App Store** (iOS) processes payment and provides purchase receipts.  
- The app stores a local flag that premium is unlocked and can **restore** purchases through the store’s APIs.  

We do **not** receive your full payment card details. Billing is handled entirely by Google or Apple under their respective privacy policies.

### 4. Data you export or import (under your control)

From **Settings → Data Management**, you may:

- **Export** your database or JSON to a file you choose  
- **Import** data from a file you select  

Export/import uses the system file picker. Files go only where **you** save or select them. We do not access those files unless you initiate import.

### 5. Information we do not collect

We do **not** intentionally collect:

- Name, email, or phone number (no account system in the app)  
- Precise location  
- Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data  
- Advertising identifiers for ad tracking  
- Analytics or crash-reporting data sent to our own services  

---

## How We Use Information

We use on-device data only to:

- Display and edit your creations  
- Remember your theme and UI preferences  
- Enable or restore premium features after a valid store purchase  
- Export or import your data when you request it  
- Check whether a newer app version is listed on the Play Store or App Store (optional “Check for updates” in Settings)

We do **not** use your content for advertising or profiling.

## Network and Third-Party Services

The app may connect to the internet only for purposes such as:

| Purpose | Third party | What is involved |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| In-app purchase & restore | Google Play Billing / Apple StoreKit | Product ID, purchase state, account used with the store |

| Open store listing | Google Play / App Store (browser or store app) | When you choose to open the store from Settings |

We are not responsible for how Google or Apple handle data under their platforms.

## Data Sharing

We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share information only:

- **With platform providers** (Google / Apple) as required for billing, restore, or store version checks, as described above  
- **When you export data** — you choose where files are saved and who receives them  
- **If required by law** — for example a valid legal request, where applicable  

We do not share your local chord database with other users or third-party marketers.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

- **On your device:** Data remains until you delete it in the app, uninstall the app, or clear app storage in system settings. Uninstalling typically removes the local database and preferences.  
- **Export files:** Retained wherever you store them; deleting those files is your responsibility.  
- **Store purchase records:** Retained according to Google’s or Apple’s policies; use **Restore** in the app or your store account to recover premium on a new device.  

To remove app data: uninstall Chord Master or clear its storage in Android/iOS system settings.

---

## Security

We rely on your device’s operating system to protect local files. No method of storage is 100% secure; keep your device locked and updated.

Because content stays on-device, **back up** important work using **Export** in Data Management if you replace your device.

---

## Children’s Privacy

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us (see below) and we will assist with removal of local data by uninstalling or clearing app storage.
